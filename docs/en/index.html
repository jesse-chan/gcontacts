Core features

Focused on contacts. Free from distractions.

After you authorize your Google Account, ContactDeck accesses data only as needed to provide its contact-management features.

01

Browse and search

Quickly view names, phone numbers, email addresses, locations, photos, and other details in your Google contacts.

02

Create and update

Add new contacts or update existing details and photos. Your changes sync to Google Contacts.

03

Organize with labels

Create, rename, and delete contact labels to keep work, friends, and important contacts organized.

✓

Data transparency

Your contacts remain under your control.

ContactDeck uses the Google People API to perform the contact actions you request. The current version does not send your Google contacts to a developer-operated backend server.

  • Google user data is never sold
  • Contact data is not used for advertising
  • You can disconnect or revoke Google access at any time
